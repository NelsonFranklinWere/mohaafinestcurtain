## 2. Google Review Acquisition via WhatsApp

Reviews are the #1 local ranking factor after your Google Business Profile. Target 50+ reviews with 4.8+ average.

### Step-by-step process

1. **After every completed installation**, send this WhatsApp message within 24 hours:

   > Hi [Name], thank you for choosing Mohaa Finest Curtains! We hope you love your new curtains. If you have a moment, a Google review helps other Nairobi homeowners find us. It takes 30 seconds: [YOUR GOOGLE REVIEW LINK] — Thank you! 🙏

2. **Get your Google Review link:**
   - Open Google Business Profile → Home → "Get more reviews"
   - Copy the short link (e.g. `https://g.page/r/XXXXX/review`)
   - Save it as a WhatsApp quick-reply shortcut

3. **Review request timing:**
   - Best time: same day as installation, when customer is happiest
   - Follow up once after 3 days if no review — do not spam

4. **Respond to every review** within 48 hours:
   - 5-star: Thank by name, mention the service (e.g. "Glad you love your blackout curtains!")
   - 3-star or below: Apologise, offer to resolve offline via WhatsApp

5. **Never offer payment for reviews** — Google will penalise your listing
